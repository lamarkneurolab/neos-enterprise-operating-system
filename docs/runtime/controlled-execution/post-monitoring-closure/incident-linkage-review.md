# Incident Linkage Review

## Purpose

Define how incidents observed during monitoring are linked and reviewed before
closure.

## Incident states

- closed
- accepted_with_residual_risk
- open
- escalated
- blocking

## Classification

Incidents must be classified by severity, affected scope, monitoring boundary,
evidence, audit linkage, rollback relationship and residual risk.

## Blocking incidents

Closure is blocked when a critical incident is open, unaudited, unresolved,
unaccepted or missing required human escalation.

## Residual risk

Residual risk may be accepted only with explicit human approval and evidence
that the risk does not exceed the closure boundary.
