# Lamark Pilot Final Document Handling Review

## Purpose

This document defines the final document handling review for a future controlled Lamark pilot.

## Document Handling Scope

The review confirms that real documents remain protected and that any future pilot request identifies document operations requiring separate authorization.

## Required Document Conditions

- No real documents may be moved without authorization.
- No real documents may be deleted without authorization.
- No real documents may be shared without authorization.
- No real documents may be transformed without authorization.
- No document permissions may be modified without authorization.
- No document content may be sent externally without authorization.
- Sandbox copies must remain clearly labeled and traceable.

## Invalid Handling Conditions

Any dependency on real document movement, deletion, sharing, transformation, permission change, or external transmission without explicit approval blocks pilot authorization.
