# Communication Connector Playbook

## Allowed preparation

Communication preparation may draft messages, build templates, classify audiences, prepare approval packets, create response trees, and document sender or recipient requirements. Drafts must remain unsent and clearly non-live.

## Execute boundary

Sending requires exact channel, sender, recipient list, message body, timing, approval, and compliance review where applicable. The approved content must match the submitted content.

## Required checks

Confirm recipient identity, channel, content finality, data classification, opt-in or relationship basis, approval record, and cancellation path before any live communication.

## Escalation triggers

Escalate when recipients are ambiguous, the message includes sensitive or regulated content, legal/compliance approval is missing, the sender identity is unclear, or the communication resembles campaign outreach.

## Block conditions

Block external communication without explicit authorization, bulk outreach, sensitive-data disclosure, credential transmission, production incident notices without approval, and campaign activation.

## Handoff evidence

The communication handoff records audience, channel, draft version, approval owner, restricted terms removed or flagged, and exact send authorization required.
